THE HISTORY OF GAMES.

Games have been a part of human culture for thousands of years. From ancient board games like Senet in Egypt to modern video games, the evolution of gaming reflects changes in technology and society. The 20th century saw the rise of arcade games, home consoles, and personal computers, leading to the diverse gaming landscape we see today.

THE TIMELINE OF GAMES

THE EVOLUTION OF GAMING TECHNOLOGY: Advancements in technology have played a crucial role in shaping the gaming industry. The transition from 2D to 3D graphics, the introduction of online multiplayer capabilities, and the development of realistic physics engines have all contributed to more immersive and engaging gaming experiences. Additionally, the rise of mobile gaming has made games more accessible to a broader audience.

THE RISE OF GAMING REVENUE VISUALISED

THE RISE OF GAMING REVENUE VISUALISED: he gaming industry has seen exponential growth in revenue over the past few decades. In 2020, the global gaming market was valued at over $159 billion, surpassing both the film and music industries. This growth is driven by factors such as increased internet connectivity, the popularity of esports, and the expansion of gaming into new markets.
